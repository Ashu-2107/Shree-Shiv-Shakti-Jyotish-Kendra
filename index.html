About The Acharya

Decades of Devotion to Vedic Science

The Guiding Light

Acharya Anuj Krishan Dubey

Acharya Anuj Krishan Dubey Ji is a highly revered Bhagwat Katha Vachak, Vyakarana Acharya, and Jyotish Acharya. With an illustrious journey spanning over 30 years, he has dedicated his life to the deep study and practical application of ancient Vedic scriptures.

Based at the Shree Shiv Shakti Jyotish Kendra in Shalimar Bagh, Delhi, Acharya Ji is not just an astrologer, but a spiritual counselor. He believes that astrology is not about fear-mongering, but rather a profound roadmap given by the divine to help us navigate life's karma with grace, awareness, and precise remedial measures.

Sacred Vedic Rituals

Rituals and mantras are the spiritual technology of our ancestors. At Shree Shiv Shakti Jyotish Kendra, Acharya Anuj Krishan Dubey and his team of highly learned Purohits conduct authentic, Parashari Vedic ceremonies.

Whether you need a simple peace-giving Havan or a grand 7-day Shrimad Bhagwat Katha, every ritual is performed with strict adherence to timing (Muhurat), purity, and deep devotion to neutralize negative karmas and invite immense prosperity.
